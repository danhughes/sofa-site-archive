Welcome to our brand new blog! We first decided to start a Sofa blog at least two years ago, but strangely enough there was always something else on our to do lists that seemed more important at the time. Thanks to the website redesign you're looking at right now, we had a good excuse to dig in and finally got it done.

On this blog we'll write about various things related to our work. You'll primarily find articles on interaction and graphic design here, topics like deciding which features make the cut and figuring out the behaviour of a new custom control. We'll occasionally post about more technical, coding-related topics and other things that seem important to us related to running our own software company, like figuring out how to get more people to try our apps or how to write a license agreement for one of our apps. But I promise that the posts about license agreements will be really rare ;)

Thanks for reading and please stay tuned, posts with actual content coming soon!
